EVERY MOUTHFUL

Food waste is a big issue. If it was a country, it’d rank as the world’s third
biggest contributor to greenhouse emissions.

By going directly to farmers, asking what they’ve got too much of and
what’s in season, we’re cutting out the supermarkets and reducing the
amount of food chucked away. Better for the planet, better for your plate.
And all those dinky apples too.
